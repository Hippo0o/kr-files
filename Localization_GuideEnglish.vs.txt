GAME_GUIDE_0118_003_DESC,"There are 31 Gear Options in total.\n\n• ATK - Affects damage dealt to enemies and the amount of heal and shield that allies receive.\n• ATK Spd - Affects the frequency of normal attacks and Skill Casting Time.\n• Crit Chance - Affects the chance to land Critical Hits on enemies.\n• Crit DMG - Affects the damage of Critical Hits.\n• Crit Resistance - Affects the chance to take Critical Hits.\n(These are classified into All Crit Resistance, P.Crit Resistance, and M.Crit Resistance.)\n• Lifesteal - Affects the chance to inflict CC, unhindered by CC Resist of enemies.\n• CC Resist - Affects the chance to resist CC.\n• Max HP - Affects the max amount of the Hero's HP.\n• Block Chance - Affects the chance to block enemy attacks.\nBlocked attacks deal 50% damage.\n(These are classified into All Block, P.Block, and M.Block.)\n\n• Block DEF - Affects the amount of damage reduced by blocking.\n(These are classified into P.Block DEF and M.Block DEF.)\n• Heal Rate - Affects the amount of healing the Hero receives.\n• DMG Reduction - Affects the amount of damage received.\n(These are classified into All DMG Reduction, P.DMG Reduction, and M.DMG Reduction.)\n• Mana Recovery/DMG - Affects the amount of Mana recovered when taking damage.\n\n▶ Block DEF, Heal Rate, DMG Reduction, and Mana Recovery/DMG can be enhanced by using Additional Options of Treasures, Runes, and through Enchantment."
